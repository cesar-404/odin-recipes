Potion of Hairy Ambitions

For Hair Growth (or Feet, depending on the luck)

This potion is revered by those seeking a luxurious mane, but beware: it has been known to take a rather literal interpretation of growth. Brew at your own risk!

Ingredients:

  • Rapunzel's Root (only use the glowing part, lest you risk an unwanted curse)
  • Pins' Hair Strands (the kind that fall from the deepest corners of your drawer, the strongest variety)
  • Dew Drops from the High Peaks (collected on a particularly windy morning)
  • A Pinch of Phoenix Feather Dust (hard to acquire, but makes everything far more interesting)
  • Laughter Tears from a Court Jester (find the best giggle you can)

Instructions:

  1. Grind the Rapunzel's Root together with the Pins' Hair Strands until you have a thick, creamy paste.
  2. Add the Dew Drops from the High Peaks while telling your best joke. If no one laughs, you might find your own tears replacing the dewdrops.
  3. Stir the mixture with the Phoenix Feather Dust and finish with the Laughter Tears.
  4. Drink the potion quickly. If all goes well, your hair will grow thicker and stronger than ever. If not, your feet may start to grow in proportion. In that case, be prepared for big shoes and even bigger stage performances.
